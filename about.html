School Seal

The School Seal of Notre Dame RVM College of Cotabato

The seal of the school is characteristically Marian. The central motif is monogram AM which means Ave Maria. “Hail Mary”, the angelic salutation which is an ejaculatory praise of the Mother of God, Mother and the Patroness of the School. The twelve stars refer to the Vision of St. John. The stars represent extraordinary privileges bestowed by God on Mary. The rays radiating from the monogram signify the graces which come to us through Mary, from Jesus, the symbolization of the congregation’s motto: “To Jesus with Mary”. Etched below the monogram is the figure of an open book upon which is inscribed the school’s motto “INITIUM SAPIENTE TIMOR DOMINI” – Fear of God is the beginning of Wisdom more...
